# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. Yaqeen is dedicated to fostering an open, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of their background, identity, or experience level.

This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all those who participate in our community, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

We invite all contributors, participants, and community members to help us create a safe and positive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior That Contributes to a Positive Environment Include:

- **Being Respectful** - Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Being Considerate** - Thinking about how your words and actions affect others
- **Being Collaborative** - Working together constructively to resolve conflicts
- **Being Patient** - Understanding that people have different knowledge levels and experiences
- **Being Professional** - Conducting yourself professionally in all interactions
- **Assuming Good Intentions** - Interpreting others' comments charitably
- **Offering Help** - Assisting fellow community members when you can
- **Providing Constructive Feedback** - Giving helpful, actionable feedback in a respectful manner
- **Celebrating Diversity** - Appreciating and valuing different perspectives and backgrounds

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior Include:

- **Harassment** - Any unwelcome conduct, comments, or actions directed at an individual or group
- **Discrimination** - Unfair treatment based on protected characteristics
- **Intimidation** - Threatening or coercive behavior
- **Abuse** - Verbal, physical, or psychological abuse
- **Exclusion** - Deliberately excluding or isolating individuals or groups
- **Hate Speech** - Speech that attacks individuals or groups based on protected characteristics
- **Sexual Harassment** - Unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, or other verbal/physical conduct of a sexual nature
- **Disrespect** - Deliberate disrespect toward individuals or groups
- **Spam** - Repeated, unsolicited messages or content
- **Trolling** - Posting inflammatory comments to provoke reactions
- **Doxxing** - Publishing private information without consent
- **Deceptive Conduct** - Deliberately misleading or dishonest behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project repositories and issue trackers
- All official project communication channels
- Project-related social media accounts
- Official project events and gatherings
- Any space where individuals are representing the project

The Code of Conduct applies to all community members, including maintainers and developers, equally.

## Enforcement

### Reporting Incidents

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, or if you have concerns about the conduct of others, please report it by contacting the project maintainers at:

**Email:** conduct@yaqeen.dev

**Include in your report:**
- Description of the incident
- People involved
- When and where it occurred
- Any relevant context or evidence
- How the incident affected you or others
- What resolution you're seeking (if any)

All reports will be treated seriously and confidentially. We will not disclose the identity of the person who reported the incident unless they request it.

### Investigation and Response

Upon receiving a report, the project maintainers will:

1. **Acknowledge Receipt** - Confirm receipt of your report within 48 hours
2. **Investigate** - Conduct a fair and thorough investigation
3. **Gather Information** - Speak with all parties involved
4. **Determine Facts** - Make an objective determination of what occurred
5. **Take Action** - Apply appropriate consequences as outlined below
6. **Notify Parties** - Inform relevant parties of the outcome

We aim to complete investigations within 2 weeks.

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior will result in consequences ranging from a warning to permanent removal from the community, depending on the severity and context:

1. **Verbal Warning** - For minor first-time offenses; a private conversation about the behavior
2. **Written Warning** - For repeated minor offenses; documented warning about continued behavior
3. **Temporary Suspension** - Temporary removal from participating in the community (typically 1-4 weeks)
4. **Permanent Ban** - Permanent removal from the community for severe or repeated violations

Factors considered in determining consequences:
- Severity of the behavior
- Whether this is a first offense or repeated behavior
- Impact on the affected person(s)
- Whether the person accepts responsibility and shows remorse
- Threat to community safety

## Appeal Process

If you believe a decision was made in error, you may appeal within 14 days of notification by:

1. Sending a written appeal to conduct@yaqeen.dev
2. Clearly stating why you believe the decision was unjust
3. Providing any additional context or evidence

Appeals will be reviewed by a separate group of project maintainers who were not involved in the initial decision.
